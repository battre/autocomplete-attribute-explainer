Australia has largely simple addresses with
  • address lines 1, 2, 3, or
  • street name, house number and up to one field for extra information (apartment number, floor, etc.).

Missing field type

The following cases are currently missing in the autocomplete spec and occurred on multiple sites:
  • street-name
  • house-number (also referred to as street no)
  • location-in-building / building-and-location-in-building (Lvl, Apt or Unit No.; Building/Floor/Apartment/Unit; Suite, building, level, etc. (optional)

Open questions

  • How do we unify "location-in-building" and "floor and/or apartment" found in Argentina? The two are probably the same, but the former is more intuitive in AU (it's sometimes paired with a building name) while the latter is more intuitive in AR.
