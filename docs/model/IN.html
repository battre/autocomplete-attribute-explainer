About the country

India has a lot of variety in address forms compared to many other countries. In particular the flat number, building name, street, landmark and "area / locality" (modeled as locality2) seem to appear in every in almost arbitrary combinations (e.g. flat number as a separate field, or grouped with building name, or grouped with building name and street, ...).

The structures below are structures we have least confidence in so far.
